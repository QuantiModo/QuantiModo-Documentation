<p dir="ltr">You agree that that your personal data that is collected by Quantimodo or posted on the Website or processed through the Services from time to time may be used for the purposes described in this policy, the PTOS and/or EUTOS.</p>
<p dir="ltr">Through the Services and Content on the Website, you may be introduced to a variety of third parties such as Practitioners and other websites. <p dir="ltr">Your personal information given through online registration, appointment making forms, feedbacks forms or any other communication channel within the site may be aggregated, but will not be disclosed on a personal basis.</p>

<h1 dir="ltr">What personal data we collect</h1>
<p dir="ltr">We will store personal data we collect from you through the account registration process, such as name, address and other contact details, other personal data you post on the Website through your use of the Services and personal data concerning you that may be posted on the Website by other users.</p>
<p dir="ltr">We may, through the use of "cookies" or other technologies, collect information about member statistics, traffic patterns and information which may include personal data such as names, addresses and emails. Moreover, we may keep details of your visits to our Website including, but not limited to, traffic data, location data, weblogs and other communication data.</p>
<p dir="ltr">We may aggregate personal and other data captured through the Website and the Services so that the data is no longer capable of identifying any individual. Aggregated data may cover patterns of usage relating to the Website and its Services (for example, analysis of Appointment transactions and the impact of the Feedback Service (as defined in the EUTOS) for End Users) and Quantimodo reserves the right to use this aggregated information for the purposes of improving and enhancing its Services, developing and selling data and information to third parties, generating and commercializing insights and otherwise for the purposes of our business.</p>
<p dir="ltr">We may also ask you for personal data when you report a problem with our site and if you contact us for any reason, we may keep a record of that correspondence.</p>

<h1 dir="ltr">What we use your data for</h1>
<p dir="ltr">The primary reason we collect personal data is to enable End Users and Registered Practitioners to engage through the Services in respect of Appointments and to enable the Content-related functions of the Website.</p>
<p dir="ltr">We may use your personal data for account and Website/Services maintenance, marketing, billing purposes and customer profiling. We use and disclose personal data relating to payment methods and details to the extent required to process fees for Completed Appointments, monthly subscriptions and other transactions.</p>
<p dir="ltr">We may collect information about your computer, including where available your IP address, operating system and browser type, for system administration and to report aggregate information to our advertisers and to generate insights.</p>
<p dir="ltr">We may use and disclose your personal data in relation to alerts to keep you updated information and other new products and services (including without limitation, third party products and services) on the Website.</p>
<p dir="ltr">We may use information about member statistics, traffic patterns for the purposes of our business activities, including sales, marketing, mergers, acquisitions, bankruptcies.</p>
<p dir="ltr">We may disclose your personal information if we are under a duty to disclose or share such data in order to comply with any legal obligation, or in order to enforce or apply the PTOS and/or EUTOS; or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Quantimodo, our customers, or others. This includes exchanging information with other companies and organizations for the purposes of fraud protection and credit risk reduction.</p>

<h1 dir="ltr">How long we keep your personal data</h1>
<p dir="ltr">We reserve the right to retain any personal data collected until your Account is terminated or for seven (7) years, whichever is the longer period.</p>

<h1 dir="ltr">Your rights of access and correction</h1>
<p dir="ltr">We will provide you with the means to ensure that your personal information is correct and current. You may review and update this information at any time. By following the "profile" link provided on our Website, you may view, edit and delete personal data you have already given us. You may also elect to receive or discontinue receiving any email newsletters we may choose to distribute periodically.</p>

<h1 dir="ltr">Changes to this policy</h1>
<p dir="ltr">Any changes we may make to this policy in the future will be posted on this Website and may be emailed to you. Changes will take effect immediately and be binding on you from the time of posting.</p>

<h1 dir="ltr">Reviews</h1>
<p dir="ltr">Keep in mind any reviews, personal information, or comments that you voluntarily disclose on the Website will become publicly available. Your account User name is displayed to other Users when you send and/or post messages through the Website. Please note that any comment or other content you post or send to the Website becomes published content and is not considered personally identifiable information subject to this Privacy Policy. Your messages and other information you make available in public areas of the Quantimodo website will be seen by people and organizations not related to or controlled by Quantimodo, and may be used by others to contact you.</p>

<h1 dir="ltr">Cookies</h1>
<p dir="ltr">The Quantimodo website may sometimes use "cookies" to personalize your online experience. The purpose of a cookie is to tell the Web server that you have returned to a specific page. Cookies cannot run programs or deliver viruses to your computer. You have the ability to accept or decline cookies by altering the settings in your web browser. Most Web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually modify your browser setting to decline cookies if you prefer. If you choose to decline cookies, you may not be able to fully experience the interactive features of the Quantimodo services or websites you visit.</p>

<h1 dir="ltr">Personal Information Security</h1>
<p dir="ltr">Quantimodo secures the personally identifiable information you provide on computer servers in a secure environment. Your information is always protected from unauthorized access. If personal information (such as a credit card number) is transmitted to other websites, its transmission is protected through the use of encryption, such as Secure Socket Layer (SSL) protection.</p>

<h1 dir="ltr">Children Under Thirteen</h1>
<p dir="ltr">Quantimodo is not directed to children under the age of thirteen, and Quantimodo will never knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under the age of thirteen. If you are under the age of thirteen, you must ask your parent or guardian for permission to visit Quantimodothis website.</p>

<p dir="ltr">Company (Quantimodo) may collect personally identifiable information, such as such as your e-mail address, name, home or work address, license or government ID and signature or telephone number for purpose of verifying identification, and to verify ownership of intellectual property. Quantimodo takes upmost care of this information, and deletes this information upon identity verification.</p>
<p dir="ltr">Quantimodo does not use or disclose, collect sensitive personal information, such as race, religion, or political affiliations, or medical information without your consent. Quantimodo keeps track of the websites and pages our customers visit within the Website, in order to determine what Website services are the most popular. This data may be used to deliver customized content and advertising within the Website to customers whose behavior indicates that they are interested in a particular subject area. In the event that Quantimodo goes through a business transaction, such as a merger or acquisition by another company, your personally identifiable information may be among the assets transferred. You will be notified via email of any such change in ownership or control of your personal information.</p>
<p dir="ltr">Quantimodo websites will disclose your personal information, without notice, only if required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to: (a) conform to the edicts of the law or comply with legal process served on Website or the site; (b) protect and defend the rights or property of Quantimodo; and, (c) act under exigent circumstances to protect the personal safety of users of Quantimodo Website, or the public.</p>
