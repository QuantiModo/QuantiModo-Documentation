## How To Register [/vc_column_text][vc_column_text] 

1.  Click the blue QuantiModo box on the main quantipress.quantimo.do page.
2.  Click "Sign Up" or effortless register with our Facebook, Google, and Twitter compatibilities.
3.  Fill out the required fields and click register
4.  Click accept when QuantiModo asks to monitor your measurements
5.  Read the pop-up message that instructs you how to get started importing data and connecting your data sources. ## How To Import Data [/vc_column_text][vc_column_text] 

1.  On the main Quantipress.quantimo.do page click "Import Data"
2.  Scroll through and click "connect" where you see an application you use.
3.  Follow the simple and easy instructions to connect with that app.
4.  Check out our data sources video (below) or click the data sources section to view compatible apps and devices.

**To import your weather data:** 
1.  Go to the import data page.
2.  Scroll down to the bottom and click connect on the weather section.
3.  Type your zip code into the box.
4.  Press enter or click "connect"
5.  Congratulations! You are now tracking humidity, cloud cover, and many other factors about your location.
